White Paper

TikStake White Paper

Introduction
The cryptocurrency revolution has brought about new opportunities and challenges for investors and everyday users alike. While the decentralized nature of digital assets such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and others has enabled global participation in financial markets, it has also exposed participants to the psychological challenges of market volatility. Many holders (so-called “HODLers”) find themselves selling too early, reacting emotionally to market swings, or falling prey to FOMO (fear of missing out), ultimately undermining their long-term wealth-building strategies.

Problem Statement
Despite widespread knowledge that a patient, long-term approach is often the most profitable strategy in the crypto space, the temptation to sell assets prematurely remains high. Many investors lack access to tools that help them enforce discipline, resulting in missed gains and regret. Existing custodial services may focus on trading or lending, but few offer specialized solutions for time-locking assets for an extended period.

Our Solution: TikStake
TikStake is a custodial crypto time-lock platform designed to empower individuals to build disciplined, long-term crypto wealth. Our platform allows users to lock their digital assets for periods ranging from 6 months to 20 years, with no promises of returns, interest, or yield generation. Instead, TikStake focuses on the psychological and practical benefits of enforced holding—removing the temptation of impulsive selling and ensuring that users stay true to their original investment goals.

How TikStake Works
1. Asset Selection: Users choose the digital asset they wish to lock—Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, or other supported coins.
2. Lock Period: Users select a lock period from a range of options, or specify a custom time frame.
3. Deposit: The platform generates a unique deposit address for the selected asset. The user sends their assets to this address.
4. Proof of Payment: Users submit the transaction hash and a payment screenshot for verification.
5. Custody: Assets are securely held in TikStake’s multi-signature, cold storage wallets. There is no lending, trading, or rehypothecation.
6. Withdrawal: After the lock period expires, users may request withdrawal. Each request is manually reviewed and processed within two days for maximum security.

Security and Transparency
Security is at the heart of TikStake. All assets are held in cold storage, protected by multi-signature access and rigorous internal controls. The platform does not engage in lending or trading with user funds, eliminating counterparty risk. Manual review of withdrawal requests adds a human layer of scrutiny, reducing the risk of unauthorized access or social engineering attacks. TikStake is committed to transparency—users can monitor their lock status, and all operational processes are clearly documented.

No Promised Returns
Many platforms entice users with the promise of interest, yield farming, or staking rewards. TikStake takes a different approach: we promise nothing but the safe custody and timely return of your original assets. Any appreciation in value during the lock period belongs entirely to the user. This approach eliminates hidden risks and aligns with the principle of true holding.

Supported Assets and Custom Requests

Withdrawal and Customer Service
Withdrawals are never automatic—all requests are manually reviewed to combat fraud and protect user security. Our customer service team is available to assist with withdrawal requests, custom lock configurations, and any concerns throughout the lock period.

Next of Kin & Security
Users are encouraged to provide a next of kin email address when submitting their deposit. In the event of the user's death, TikStake will use this information to help ensure that your crypto can be securely transferred to your designated beneficiary, after proper verification. Providing your own email and next of kin details is completely optional but strongly recommended for long-term peace of mind.
