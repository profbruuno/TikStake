Project Overview

TikStake is a custodial crypto-locking platform for users who struggle with long-term holding. Select a lock duration, send your coins, and we keep them secure. Market gains or losses go entirely to you—no returns promised.

About Us

Founded in Helsinki, 2025, TikStake unites blockchain security engineers and financial strategists. Our mission is to help you stay the course in crypto by enforcing discipline through time-locks.
